Rafting Travel

Rafting Travel an adventure company. Our purpose is to offer our clients safe rafting trips, in stunning places, with the best quality equipment and support from experienced and highly trained professionals.Our mission is to offer our customers close contact with nature, exciting experiences and lots of fun, with the security of assistance from qualified professionals and at a fair price. Our moto is, Rafting Travel adventure and joy your way!

History

Ten years on the market, our company started in 2014 in the city of Natal, the beautiful capital of the Brazilian state of Rio Grande do Norte. From there we expanded until in 2020 we reached all cities in the northeast region of Brazil.

From 2021 we began our adventure in the beautiful city of Rio de Janeiro, from where we headed to Minas Gerais, Espírito Santo and finally São Paulo. This growth is motivated by the love we feel for what we do, quality of service and a dream that never ceases to inspire us.
